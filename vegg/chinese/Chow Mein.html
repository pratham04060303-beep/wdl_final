🥢 🍜 Chow Mein Classic Chinese Noodles

Spring Rolls

Crispy Indo-Chinese Starter

About Dish

Chow Mein is a flavorful noodle dish made with stir-fried vegetables and sauces cooked over high flame.

Ingredients

Boil noodles Heat oil Carrot Capsicum Soy Sauce Chilli Sauce Garlic Oil Salt

Steps (Click for details)

1. Boil noodles
Finely chop cabbage, carrot, capsicum and spring onions.
2. Heat oil
Heat oil in a wok and stir fry garlic and vegetables on high flame.
3.Stir fry garlic
Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, salt and pepper. Mix well.
4. Add vegetables
5. Add sauces
6.Toss noodles
7. Garnish
8. Serve hot
Let the cooked filling cool completely before rolling.
5. Roll the Spring Rolls
Place filling inside the sheet and roll tightly using flour slurry to seal.
6. Deep Fry
Fry the rolls in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
7. Serve Hot
Serve hot with schezwan sauce or sweet chilli sauce.
