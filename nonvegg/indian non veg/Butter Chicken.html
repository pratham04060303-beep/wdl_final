Butter Chicken

Steps (Click for details)

1. Marinate Chicken
Mix chicken with yogurt and spices, keep for 30 minutes.
2. Cook Chicken
Grill or fry the marinated chicken until cooked.
3. Prepare Gravy
Heat butter, cook onions, ginger-garlic paste, and tomato puree.
4. Add Spices
Add chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt.
5. Mix Chicken
Add cooked chicken to gravy and mix well.
6. Add Cream
Pour fresh cream and cook for a few minutes.
7. Serve Hot
Serve with naan, roti, or rice.
