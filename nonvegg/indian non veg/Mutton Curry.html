🥘 Mutton Curry

Mutton Curry

Steps (Click for details)

1. Marinate the Mutton
Wash mutton pieces thoroughly. Marinate with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric, mutton masala and salt. Keep for at least 1 hour or overnight for best results.
2. Fry the Onions
Heat oil in a pressure cooker or heavy pot. Add finely chopped onions and fry on medium heat until deep golden brown. This is the key to a rich dark gravy.
3. Add Ginger-Garlic & Tomatoes
Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes until raw smell disappears. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and oil separates.
4. Add Spices
Add chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder and mutton masala. Mix well and cook the masala on medium heat for 4–5 minutes until fragrant.
5. Add Mutton & Cook
Add the marinated mutton pieces and mix well with the masala. Cook on high heat for 5 minutes, stirring frequently to seal in the juices.
6. Pressure Cook
Add 1 cup water, close the lid and pressure cook for 5–6 whistles on medium flame. Let pressure release naturally. Open and check if mutton is tender.
7. Garnish & Serve
Sprinkle garam masala and fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with roti, naan, steamed rice or bhakri.
