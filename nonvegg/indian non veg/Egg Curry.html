Egg Curry

Steps (Click for details)

1. Boil the Eggs
Boil eggs in water for 10 minutes. Peel them and make 2–3 shallow slits on each egg so the masala soaks in well.
2. Fry the Eggs
Heat oil in a pan. Lightly fry the boiled eggs with a pinch of turmeric and salt until golden on the outside. Set aside.
3. Cook the Onions
In the same pan, add more oil. Fry finely chopped onions on medium heat until golden brown.
4. Add Ginger-Garlic & Tomatoes
Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes. Then add chopped tomatoes or tomato puree and cook until soft and oil separates.
5. Add Spices
Add chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder and salt. Mix well and cook the masala on medium heat for 3–4 minutes.
6. Add Eggs to Gravy
Add the fried eggs into the masala. Pour in a little water to adjust gravy consistency. Simmer for 5–7 minutes on low flame.
7. Garnish & Serve
Sprinkle garam masala and fresh coriander leaves on top. Serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice.
