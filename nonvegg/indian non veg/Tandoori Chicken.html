Tandoori Chicken

Steps (Click for details)

1. Clean & Score Chicken
Clean chicken pieces thoroughly. Make deep cuts/slits on the chicken with a knife so the marinade penetrates deep into the meat for maximum flavour.
2. First Marinade
Rub chicken with lemon juice, chilli powder, turmeric and salt. Let it rest for 20–30 minutes. This tenderises the meat and removes any raw smell.
3. Second Marinade
Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, tandoori masala, garam masala, coriander powder and oil into a smooth paste. Coat the chicken thoroughly and marinate for at least 4–6 hours or overnight in the fridge.
4. Preheat Oven / Grill
Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F) or heat a grill/tawa on high flame. Brush the grill rack with oil to prevent sticking.
5. Grill the Chicken
Place chicken on the grill rack or oven tray. Cook for 20–25 minutes, turning halfway through. Baste with butter while cooking for a smoky charred finish.
6. Char for Smoky Flavour
In the last 3–4 minutes, switch to broil/high flame to get the signature charred and smoky tandoori look on the outside.
7. Garnish & Serve
Squeeze fresh lemon juice on top and garnish with sliced onion rings and coriander. Serve hot with mint chutney, naan or roomali roti.
