Steps (Click for details)

1. Clean & Marinate Fish
Clean fish pieces thoroughly. Marinate with turmeric, chilli powder, salt and a little lemon juice for 20–30 minutes.
2. Fry the Fish
Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the marinated fish until lightly golden on both sides. Remove and set aside.
3. Cook the Onions
In the same pan, add oil and fry finely chopped onions on medium heat until golden brown and soft.
4. Add Ginger-Garlic & Tomatoes
Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy and oil separates from the masala.
5. Add Spices & Tamarind
Add chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder and fish curry masala. Mix well. Add tamarind water for a tangy flavour and cook for 3–4 minutes.
6. Add Coconut Milk & Fish
Pour in coconut milk and stir gently. Add the fried fish pieces. Simmer on low flame for 8–10 minutes so the fish absorbs all the flavours.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with steamed rice, appam or roti.
