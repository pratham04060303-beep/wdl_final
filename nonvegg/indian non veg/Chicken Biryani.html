Chicken Biryani

Restaurant Style Recipe

Steps (Click for details)

1. Marinate Chicken
Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric, and biryani masala. Marinate for at least 1 hour.
2. Fry Onions
Heat oil in a pan and fry thinly sliced onions until golden brown and crispy. Set aside half for garnish.
3. Cook Chicken
In the same pan, cook the marinated chicken on medium heat until nearly done. Add fried onions and mix well.
4. Parboil Rice
Boil basmati rice with whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom) and salt until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
5. Layer Biryani
In a heavy pot, layer chicken at the bottom, then rice on top. Add mint leaves, coriander, fried onions and saffron milk.
6. Dum Cooking
Seal the pot with a tight lid or dough. Cook on low flame for 25–30 minutes so the biryani cooks in its own steam.
7. Serve Hot
Gently mix and serve hot with raita, mirchi ka salan, and sliced onions.
