Chicken Tamales

Restaurant Style Recipe

1. Soak the Corn Husks
Place the dried corn husks in a large bowl or basin. Cover completely with warm water and soak for at least 30–45 minutes until they are soft and pliable. Weigh them down with a plate if needed. Drain and pat dry before using.
2. Cook & Shred the Chicken
Boil chicken pieces in salted water with garlic, onion and cumin for 20–25 minutes until fully cooked. Remove chicken, let it cool slightly, then shred it with two forks into thin strips. Reserve the broth for the masa dough.
3. Make the Chicken Filling
Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté chopped onion and garlic for 3–4 minutes. Add tomato puree, chilli flakes, cumin, salt and black pepper. Stir in the shredded chicken and cook for 5 minutes until the filling is well coated and slightly dry. Set aside to cool.
4. Prepare the Masa Dough
In a large bowl, beat softened butter until fluffy. Gradually mix in masa harina, warm chicken broth, salt and a pinch of cumin. Knead into a smooth, spreadable dough — it should hold its shape but not be too stiff. Add more broth if needed. A small ball of dough should float in water when ready.
5. Assemble the Tamales
Lay a soaked corn husk flat on a surface. Spread a thin layer of masa dough (about 3–4 mm thick) over the wide end, leaving a 2 cm border on the sides. Place 1–2 tbsp of chicken filling in the centre. Fold the sides of the husk over the filling, then fold the narrow bottom end up. Tie with a strip of husk or leave folded side down.
6. Steam the Tamales
Place a steamer rack in a large pot with 2–3 cm of water at the bottom. Stand the tamales upright with the open end facing up, packed tightly so they hold each other. Cover with a layer of extra corn husks and a damp cloth. Steam on medium heat for 60–75 minutes. Add more water if needed. Tamales are done when the masa peels cleanly away from the husk.
7. Rest & Serve
Remove tamales from the steamer and let them rest for 5–10 minutes — they firm up as they cool. Unwrap and serve hot with fresh salsa, sour cream, chopped coriander and a squeeze of lemon. Enjoy fresh or store and reheat by steaming again for 10 minutes.
