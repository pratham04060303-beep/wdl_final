Prawn Spring Rolls

Restaurant Style Recipe

Steps (Click for details)

1. Clean & Chop Prawns
Peel, devein and finely chop the prawns into small pieces. Pat dry with paper towel. Season with ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, black pepper and salt. Rest for 15 minutes so the flavours absorb well.
2. Stir-Fry the Prawns
Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add the marinated prawns and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until pink and just cooked through. Remove and set aside. Do not overcook or they will become rubbery.
3. Cook the Vegetables
In the same wok, add a little oil. Stir-fry shredded cabbage, julienned carrot, chopped capsicum and spring onion whites on high flame for 2–3 minutes. Keep the vegetables slightly crunchy for texture.
4. Season the Filling
Add the cooked prawns back to the vegetables. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce and black pepper. Toss everything together well. Let the filling cool completely before rolling — hot filling makes the wrapper soggy.
5. Roll the Spring Rolls
Lay a spring roll wrapper flat. Place 2 tablespoons of prawn filling near one edge. Fold in the sides and roll tightly. Seal the open edge with a paste of maida and water to prevent it from opening during frying.
6. Fry Until Golden
Heat oil in a deep pan on medium-high flame. Gently slide in the spring rolls and fry in batches for 3–4 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are deep golden and crispy all around.
7. Serve Hot
Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil. Serve immediately with sweet chilli sauce, schezwan chutney or soy dipping sauce on the side.
