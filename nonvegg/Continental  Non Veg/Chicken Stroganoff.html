Chicken Stroganoff

Restaurant Style Recipe

1. Prepare the Chicken
Cut chicken breast into thin strips about 1 cm wide. Season generously with salt and black pepper. Dust lightly with flour and toss to coat evenly. The flour coating helps the chicken brown beautifully and also thickens the sauce.
2. Sear the Chicken
Heat olive oil and butter in a wide pan over high heat. Add the chicken strips in a single layer — do not crowd the pan. Sear for 2–3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove and set aside. Searing in batches if needed ensures browning not steaming.
3. Sauté Onion & Garlic
In the same pan reduce heat to medium. Add a little more butter. Add finely chopped onion and cook for 3–4 minutes until softened and translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Cook the Mushrooms
Add sliced mushrooms to the pan. Cook on medium-high heat for 4–5 minutes until the mushrooms release their moisture and turn golden brown. Do not stir too much — let them brown properly for the best flavour.
5. Deglaze & Make the Sauce
Pour in white wine or chicken stock and scrape up all the browned bits from the bottom of the pan — these add incredible depth of flavour. Let it bubble for 2 minutes to reduce slightly. Reduce heat to low.
6. Add Cream & Chicken
Stir in sour cream or fresh cream. Mix gently until the sauce is smooth and creamy. Return the seared chicken strips to the pan. Toss everything together and simmer on low heat for 5 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce coats everything beautifully.
7. Garnish & Serve
Season with extra salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley. Serve hot over egg noodles, steamed rice, mashed potatoes or with crusty garlic bread on the side.
