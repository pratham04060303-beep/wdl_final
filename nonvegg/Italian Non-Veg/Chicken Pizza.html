Chicken Pizza

1. Prepare the Pizza Dough
Mix maida, yeast, salt, sugar, olive oil and warm water into a soft dough. Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour until doubled in size. If using store-bought dough, skip this step.
2. Marinate & Cook Chicken
Cut chicken into small pieces. Marinate with garlic, chilli flakes, black pepper, olive oil and salt. Cook in a pan on medium-high heat for 5–6 minutes until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
3. Make the Pizza Sauce
Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add tomato puree, salt, chilli flakes and dried oregano. Simmer on low heat for 8–10 minutes until the sauce thickens. Let it cool.
4. Roll Out the Dough
Punch down the risen dough and roll it out into a round circle on a floured surface to about 0.5 cm thickness. Transfer to a greased baking tray or pizza stone. Prick the base with a fork to prevent air bubbles.
5. Assemble the Pizza
Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base leaving a 1 cm border for the crust. Sprinkle a generous layer of grated mozzarella cheese. Top with cooked chicken pieces, sliced onion and capsicum. Add chilli flakes and oregano on top.
6. Bake the Pizza
Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F). Bake the assembled pizza for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and crispy and the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned on top.
7. Slice & Serve
Remove from oven and let it rest for 2 minutes. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle fresh basil or oregano. Slice and serve hot immediately.
