Chicken Pasta

Steps (Click for details)

1. Boil the Pasta
Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook pasta until al dente as per packet instructions. Reserve half a cup of pasta water before draining. Toss the drained pasta with a little olive oil to prevent sticking.
2. Cook the Chicken
Season chicken pieces with salt, black pepper and chilli flakes. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook chicken on medium-high heat for 5–6 minutes until golden and cooked through. Slice or shred and set aside.
3. Sauté Onion & Garlic
In the same pan, heat olive oil. Add finely chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant and lightly golden.
4. Add Capsicum & Tomatoes
Add diced capsicum and cook for 2 minutes. Pour in tomato puree and mix well. Cook the sauce on medium heat for 5–7 minutes until it thickens slightly and the raw tomato smell disappears.
5. Make it Creamy
Lower the flame and stir in fresh cream. Add a little reserved pasta water to adjust the consistency. Season with salt, black pepper and chilli flakes. Simmer for 2 minutes until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
6. Add Pasta & Chicken
Add the boiled pasta and cooked chicken to the sauce. Toss gently until every piece of pasta is well coated. Add grated cheese and mix well so it melts into the sauce.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with fresh basil or parsley leaves and extra grated cheese. Serve hot immediately with garlic bread on the side.
