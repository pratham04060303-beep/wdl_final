Chicken Piccata

Restaurant Style Recipe

1. Prepare the Chicken
Place chicken breasts between plastic wrap and pound them to an even thickness of about 1 cm. This ensures even cooking. Season both sides generously with salt and black pepper.
2. Coat with Flour
Lightly dredge each chicken breast in flour, shaking off any excess. The flour coating creates a light golden crust and helps thicken the sauce later.
3. Pan-Fry the Chicken
Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium-high heat. Cook the floured chicken for 3–4 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and set aside on a plate.
4. Sauté the Garlic
In the same pan, reduce heat to medium. Add a little butter and sauté minced garlic for 1 minute until fragrant. Scrape up any browned bits from the pan — these add great flavour to the sauce.
5. Make the Piccata Sauce
Add chicken stock or cream to the pan. Squeeze in fresh lemon juice and add lemon zest. Stir in capers. Let the sauce simmer for 3–4 minutes until it reduces slightly and becomes glossy.
6. Finish with Butter
Swirl in a tablespoon of cold butter to make the sauce silky and rich. Return the chicken to the pan and spoon the sauce over it. Simmer for 2 minutes so the chicken absorbs all the lemony flavour.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and lemon slices. Serve hot with pasta, mashed potatoes or crusty garlic bread on the side.
