Chilli Chicken

Restaurant Style Recipe

About Dish

Steps (Click for details)

1. Marinate the Chicken
Cut chicken into small cubes. Marinate with ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, chilli powder, black pepper, salt and cornflour. Mix well and rest for 20–30 minutes.
2. Fry the Chicken
Heat oil in a wok or deep pan. Deep fry the marinated chicken pieces on high heat for 4–5 minutes until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels and set aside.
3. Stir-Fry Aromatics
In a separate wok, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add chopped garlic, green chillies and the white part of spring onion. Stir-fry on high flame for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
4. Add Onion & Capsicum
Add diced onion and capsicum. Toss on high heat for 2–3 minutes keeping them slightly crunchy. The high flame gives the dish a nice smoky wok flavour.
5. Add Sauces
Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, a pinch of sugar and black pepper. Mix well. If you want a dry version stop here. For a semi-gravy version, mix 1 teaspoon cornflour in water and add it to the wok.
6. Toss the Chicken
Add the fried chicken to the wok. Toss everything together on high flame for 2–3 minutes so every piece is coated in the glossy, spicy sauce.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with chopped spring onion greens. Serve hot as a starter or pair with chicken fried rice or hakka noodles.
