Chicken Spring Rolls

Restaurant Style Recipe

About Dish

Ingredients

Steps (Click for details)

1. Prepare the Filling
Heat oil in a wok. Add ginger-garlic paste and stir-fry for 1 minute. Add minced or shredded chicken and cook on high heat for 4–5 minutes until done.
2. Add Vegetables
Add shredded cabbage, julienned carrot, chopped capsicum and spring onion to the wok. Stir-fry on high flame for 2–3 minutes keeping the vegetables slightly crunchy.
3. Season the Filling
Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, black pepper and salt. Toss everything together well. Let the filling cool completely before rolling — this prevents the wrapper from getting soggy.
4. Prepare Spring Roll Wrappers
Use readymade spring roll wrappers or make thin crepes using maida, water and a pinch of salt. Keep them covered with a damp cloth so they don't dry out.
5. Roll the Spring Rolls
Place a wrapper on a flat surface. Add 2 tablespoons of filling near one edge. Fold the sides in and roll tightly. Seal the edge with a paste of maida and water to prevent opening while frying.
6. Fry Until Golden
Heat oil in a deep pan on medium-high flame. Fry the spring rolls in batches for 3–4 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy all over.
7. Serve Hot
Drain on paper towels. Serve immediately with sweet chilli sauce, schezwan chutney or tomato ketchup on the side.
