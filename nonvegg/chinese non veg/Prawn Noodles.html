Prawn Noodles

Restaurant Style Recipe

Steps (Click for details)

1. Boil the Noodles
Boil noodles in salted water as per packet instructions until just cooked (al dente). Drain, rinse with cold water and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking. Set aside.
2. Clean & Marinate Prawns
Clean and devein the prawns. Marinate with ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, black pepper and salt for 15 minutes so the flavours soak in well.
3. Cook the Prawns
Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add the marinated prawns and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until pink and just cooked through. Do not overcook or they will become rubbery. Remove and set aside.
4. Stir-Fry Vegetables
In the same wok, add more oil. Add ginger-garlic paste, spring onion whites, shredded cabbage, julienned carrot and capsicum. Stir-fry on high flame for 2–3 minutes keeping vegetables crunchy.
5. Add Noodles & Sauces
Add the boiled noodles to the wok. Pour in soy sauce, chilli sauce, black pepper and salt. Toss everything together on high flame for 2 minutes so the noodles are evenly coated in the sauce.
6. Add Prawns Back
Add the cooked prawns back into the wok. Toss gently with the noodles on high heat for 1–2 minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with chopped spring onion greens. Serve hot immediately with extra chilli sauce or schezwan chutney on the side.
