Chicken Fried Rice

Restaurant Style Recipe

Steps (Click for details)

1. Cook & Cool the Rice
Cook basmati rice until just done. Spread it on a plate and let it cool completely. Day-old rice works best as it is drier and gives better fried rice texture.
2. Cook the Chicken
Cut chicken into small pieces. Season with salt, pepper and a little soy sauce. Stir-fry in a hot wok with oil on high heat for 5–6 minutes until cooked through. Set aside.
3. Scramble the Eggs
In the same wok, add a little oil. Beat eggs and pour them in. Scramble on high heat until just set but still soft. Push to the side of the wok.
4. Stir-Fry Vegetables
Add oil and stir-fry chopped onion, ginger-garlic paste, capsicum and the white part of spring onion on high flame for 2–3 minutes. Keep the heat high for a smoky flavour.
5. Add Rice & Sauces
Add the cooled rice to the wok. Toss well on high flame. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, salt and black pepper. Mix everything together quickly so the rice does not clump.
6. Add Chicken & Egg
Add the cooked chicken pieces and scrambled egg back into the wok. Toss everything together on high heat for 2 minutes until well combined and heated through.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with chopped spring onion greens. Serve hot with chicken manchurian, chilli chicken or on its own with extra chilli sauce on the side.
