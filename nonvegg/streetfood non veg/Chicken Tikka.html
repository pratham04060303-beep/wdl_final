Chicken Tikka Masala

Restaurant Style Recipe

About Dish

Ingredients

Steps (Click for details)

1. Marinate Chicken
Mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chilli powder, turmeric, tikka masala and salt. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
2. Grill the Tikka
Skewer the marinated chicken and grill or bake at 220°C for 20–25 minutes until charred and cooked through. You can also cook on a tawa with butter.
3. Prepare the Base
Heat butter in a pan. Add chopped onions and cook until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes.
4. Add Tomatoes & Spices
Add tomato puree, chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric and garam masala. Cook on medium heat until oil separates from the masala.
5. Blend the Gravy
Let the gravy cool slightly, then blend it smooth. Pass through a strainer for a silky restaurant-style texture.
6. Add Chicken & Cream
Add the grilled tikka pieces to the gravy. Pour in fresh cream and mix gently. Simmer for 5–7 minutes on low flame.
7. Garnish & Serve
Garnish with a swirl of cream and fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with butter naan, roti or steamed rice.
